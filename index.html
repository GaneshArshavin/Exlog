About Us

What is Our Goal?

The objective of the SSN MATH CLUB is to motivate the students to apply the various mathematical techniques in their respective field and to promote more mathematical knowledge applicable to Engineering and Technology among students. SSN MATH CLUB is formed to enrich the quantitative aptitude of the students. Eminent professors are invited to deliver guest lectures at regular intervals. The motivation for starting SSN MATH CLUB is to provide a platform for the inquiring mind to research into unfamiliar areas and learn new concepts in a fun and recreational context. Club exists to encourage, challenge, and develop the skills and enjoyment of anyone with an interest in the fascinating world of mathematics.
