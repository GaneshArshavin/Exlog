EVENT NAME : CREODEL

ABOUT :

    Need a breather from the banality of college life? Here’s an event which requires a dash of creativity and a splash of zest. Put your Math knowledge and Bob the Builder skills into use! And guess what? You have chances of winning exciting cash prizes too!

ROUNDS AND RULES :

    ROUND 1:

    • A combination of shapes will be given and the equivalent volume must be found.

    ROUND 2:

    • A model has to be built to solve the given trigonometric problem.

    ROUND 3:

    • A set of equations will be given and their solutions must be put together to solve the overall puzzle to build a model.

    RULES:

    • A team can comprise 2-3 members.
    • Strictly no use of internet. Your brains are the only source of information you can use.
    • Materials required for model making will be provided.
    • Explanation must be provided for the derived answer.
    • Judging will be based on time management and neatness.
