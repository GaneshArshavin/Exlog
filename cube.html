EVENT NAME: BIGG BOSS

ABOUT :

    The game starts by assigning participants to rooms whose members will be the participant's new family for the next few hours. The participant needs to wn immunity to proceed to the next round. This can either be by winning assigned tasks or winning family vote to stay with them. This is a game of creating and breaking bonds for survival to become the ultimate BIGG BOSS!

ROUNDS AND RULES:

    ROUNDS:

    • Number of rounds: 4
    • Each round duration : 20-35 mins ( Including voting time depending on number of participants )
    • WILD CARD ENTRY and GOLDEN TICKET will be after round 2.

    RULES:

    • Violent behaviour will not be encouraged.
    • Watches,calculators( electronic / solar ),phones,smart watches,etc. or any other electronic devices will not be allowed inside the premise.
    • Decision of BIGG BOSS will be final.
    • Participants should be present inside the room until eliminated.
    • Participants should participate in all tasks given by BIGG BOSS.
    • Only limited number of participants will be welcomed.
