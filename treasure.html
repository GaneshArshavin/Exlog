EVENT NAME: TREASURE HUNT

ABOUT :

    Have you ever enjoyed the scenic view of SSN? Our Treasure Hunt brings you an exclusive shot at this panorama, where you explore new locations with clues from solving cool Maths game. The first team to reach the end of the journey walks off with the treasure!

ROUNDS AND RULES:

    PRELIMINARY ROUND:

    • A team must consist of minimum of 2 members and maximum of 3 members.
    • Depending on the number of teams registered, the preliminary round will be conducted.
    • This round will have 20 general aptitude questions, from which top 10 tems will qualify to the next round.

    FINAL ROUND:

    • Selected Finalists will be given a set of questions to be solved.
    • On completion of it, each team will get a clue that directs to a spot inside the college.
    • The same procedure will be repeated at each spot till your final spot.
    • The team which finishes it in minimum duration gets the first place.
    • In case of no such team, the winner will be decided based on the total number of questions answered in each spot.

    RULES:

    • Students are allowed to use calculators but not mobile phones.
    • Students will be provided with 2 swap cards and can opt to swap their current question twice during the game.
    • Any kind of malpractice will lead to the disqualification of the team.
