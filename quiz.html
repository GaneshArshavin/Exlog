EVENT NAME: QUIZ

ABOUT :

    To all the Maths Wizards out there! This is the place for you to venture into the world of Mathematics, put your knowledge and skills to test and grab exciting prizes. It's a 2 round event comprising of a pen-paper and a quiz style round

ROUNDS AND RULES:

    ROUND 1: PRELIMS

    • 20 questions in total
    • Time limit : 30 minutes
    • 10 questions from aptitude and 10 from general plus a bonus question. All questions are multiple choice except the bonus question.
    • Each correct answer earns your team 2 points. Aptitude section carries negative marking.
    • Maximum possible score : 45
    • Top 6-8 teams will qualify for the finals.
    • Quiz master's decision is final and binding

    ROUND 2: FINALS

    • The Final round will consist of 6 rounds containing 2 rounds each from general and aptitude section and 2 special rounds
    • Other rules will be intimated on the spot

    RULES:

    • A team can consist maximum of 2 members.
    • The participants are not allowed to use mobile phones or any other electronic instruments.
