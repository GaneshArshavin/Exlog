EVENT NAME: PANTOMIME

ABOUT :

    Actions speak louder than words.Tweaking it to our convenience,we challenge your charades to speak louder than words and stun us with your ‘dumb’ act at EXLOG’s “DUMB C” event.

ROUNDS AND RULES:

    ROUND 1: APTITUDE TEST

    • A team must consist of minimum 2 members and maximum 3 members.
    • An general math aptitude test will be your first round and the participants will appear as a team here.

    ROUND 2: DUMB-C

    • Only one person from each team should come forward to get the chit containing a list of five mathematical terms which they should act in the minimum possible time.Should not exceed 3 mins.
    • The person acting should not speak anything from his/her mouth ,should not point to any object or person and should convey only through actions.
    • Only one split per word is allowed.

    ROUND 3: 4 PICTURES 1 WORD

    • The team as a whole should come out with the most appropriate word relating the displayed four pictures.
    • Teams finding the maximum no. of words in 90 seconds will be given the first three positions.
